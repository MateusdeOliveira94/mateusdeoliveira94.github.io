    About me

    From Architecture to Digital Design

    Bachelor’s in Architecture and Urban Design turned UX/UI Designer. After years of designing physical spaces, I discovered my passion for designing journeys in the digital world — accessible, functional, and purposeful. I believe in empathy-driven design, grounded in research and real-world data, and focused on products that not only delight users but also drive business goals.

    Design Philosophy

    • Human-Centered Design: I study human behavior to minimize cognitive load and create intuitive flows.
    • Minimalism with purpose: I prioritize simplicity, accessibility, and clarity in my designs.
    • Teamwork & Diversity: As a neurodivergent and queer person, I believe the best solutions come from collaborative, inclusive environments.
